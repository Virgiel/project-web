Principle

The process of fitness selection starts with the simulation of a generation of entities. At the end of a generation (a timeout or the end of a task) a new generation is made of the fittest individuals from the population. New entities inherit their parent's characteristics. If parents have good fitness, their babies will inherit it and have a better chance of surviving. This process keeps on iterating and in the end, a generation with the fittest individuals will be found.

During the inheritance of characteristics, some mutations are applied with a low random probability. Mutations occur to create new possibilities and find new solutions but a huge mutation rate damage the learning rate.

Genetic AI can be applied to a search problem. We consider a set of solutions for a problem and select the set of best ones out of them.
